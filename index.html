Who am I?

Welcome to my home page. I have been working as a professor of the Robotics course at the Santo André campus of Federal University of ABC.

Research Interests

  • Transfer Learning
  • Reinforcement Learning
  • Robotics

What am I working on?

  • Coordination of the Robotic Intelligent System (RIS) research group at UFABC.
  • Coordination of the UFABC Robotics team.
  • RobocarRACE – project to build autonomous vehicles in scale, helping to improve the research of autonomous vehicles in Brazil.
  • Project Aster – focused on flight missions to near-Earth asteroids.
