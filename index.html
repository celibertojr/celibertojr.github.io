Who am I?

Welcome to my homepage. I am a professor in the Robotics program at the Santo André campus of the Federal University of ABC (UFABC).

Research Interests

  • Transfer Learning
  • Reinforcement Learning
  • Robotics
  • Multi-Agent Systems

Current Activities

  • Coordinator of the Robotic Intelligent Systems research group (RIS)
  • Founder and Coordinator of the UFABC Robotics Team
  • Coordinator and founding member of RobocarRACE in Brazil, a project focused on developing scaled autonomous vehicles to advance research in autonomous driving. (www.roborace.com.br)
  • Project Aster: Flight to a near-Earth asteroid (Aster Mission Infrared Spectrometer)

Previous Experience

  • Vice-General Chair, Brazilian Robotics Competition (2012–2013)
  • IEEE Coordinator, Latin American Robotics Competition (2010)
